SECTION 7: Claims Procedure

1. Upon mechanical breakdown, contact the Administrator immediately at 1-844-809-5829 during the hours of 9:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m. EST, Monday thru Friday. In the event the office is closed or for emergency repairs during non-business hours, leave a message and if You so choose, have Your vehicle towed to the nearest repairfacility or a facility of Your choice. Please provide your name, contract number, description of the problem, current mileage, and your phone to bereached the next business day. The Administrator will return Your call immediately the next business day.
2. Once the vehicle is at the repair facility, have the repair facility call the Administrator at the number above to obtain the necessary authorization to begin work onthe Vehicle. Make sure that neither You nor the repair facility start work on the vehicle without prior authorization. Work started before the proper authorization will delay Your claim or VOID Your claim.
3. Once authorization has been granted, the repair facility will perform diagnostics. Should the problem with the vehicle be covered under this Contract,We will cover all applicable diagnostic charges up to 1.5 hours at the contract labor rate. In the event the mechanical breakdown was caused for a reason not covered under this Contract, You will be responsible for all applicable charges owed to the repair facility. We will not cover misdiagnosis orrepairs done for process of elimination purposes to reach correct diagnosis. Any such paid repairs will be deducted from future approvals relating tothat repair.
4. After the claim has approval for repair, the Administrator will call the repair facility and issue an approval code. Approval will be based on the following criteria:

a. Parts: We reserve the right to use parts in OEM, reproduction, manufactured, rebuilt form, or parts of like kind and quality. It is necessary that the repair facility hold on to the replaced parts until the claim is paid in full. In some cases, we may request visual inspection of the old parts for quality control purposes. We will pay up to $69.95 for Wheel Alignments in conjunction with covered repairs. We will pay up to $50 for fluids in conjunction with a covered repair.
b. Labor: Labor costs will be determined using Mitchell 1. This labor guide is used by mechanics nationwide. We pay an hourly labor rate of $85.00 perhour of the posted labor rate. Any amount over that rate is the responsibility of the Contract Holder. We will pay an hourly labor rate of up to $126.00 per hour of the posted labor rate for E, X, Y, and Z Class vehicles. The Class of your vehicle is listed on the registration page of this Contract.
5. Major Claims: In the event the claim is extensive, We may send out an inspector. The inspectors are ASE certified mechanics and are sometimes necessary to document the claim. If an engine needs to be rebuilt, we reserve the right to send the engine to a machine shop of our choice if we deem necessary.
6. Start of Repair Work: Once all the above information has been compiled, a claims adjuster will then review the information and determine an amount to authorize for the repair. The amount will be broken down between parts and labor. The claims adjuster will then notify the repair facility and issue an approval number along with the amount broken down accordingly.

7. Completion of Work: Once the work has been completed, the repair facility must send a copy of the final itemized bill that includes your signature. Your signature on this final itemized bill is confirmation that the repair has been completed and you are satisfied with the repair and the quality of the workmanship. Once this signed copy has been returned, along with the proper payment cover sheet, payment will be released to your repair facility. Please note any calls may be recorded for quality control purposes. After the above-mentioned steps have been completed, we will pay the repair facility the agreed upon amount.

8. When a claim is paid, You are required to pay the deductible chosen by You and specified on the Registration Page of this Contract. The deductible applies to each claim filed.
9. Freedom Warranty must receive a signed final invoice on all approved claims within 90 days from the approval date. Failure to submit the final invoice by either the Repair Facility or You within the 90 days will result in a cancelled claim. If You have financed the purchase of this Vehicle Service Contract through Our finance company and there is an outstanding balance due at the time of the claim and the amount of the claim is greater than the amount You owe on Your contract, the balance You owe on Your contract must be paid in full to reflect a zero-balance owed.
